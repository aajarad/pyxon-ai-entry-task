Artificial Intelligence in the Modern World

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science focused on developing systems that can perform tasks requiring processes that resemble human thinking.

Types of Artificial Intelligence:

1. Narrow AI: Specializes in specific tasks like image recognition or translation.

2. General AI: Can perform any task that a human can do.

Applications of AI:

- Machine Translation
- Image Recognition
- Medical Diagnosis
- Education and Smart Teaching
- Financial Analysis and Prediction
- Business Services and Accounting

Challenges and Opportunities:

The world faces challenges in adopting AI technologies, such as:
- Lack of trained data
- Language complexity
- Need for specialized models

However, there are significant opportunities for developing and using AI in various fields.
